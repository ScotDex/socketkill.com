PROJECT GENESIS

Socket.Kill is a high-performance front end inspired by the classic retro-futuristic CRT monitor presentation from the Alien franchise. Born from a specialized concept involving discarded data, the focus is pure performance. Minimal overhead, a centralized image proxy, and localized caching keep parsing performance under 100ms.

VS. ZKILLBOARD

It isn't designed to challenge zKill's rich historical depth. It is a shorthand tool optimized for rendering speed over rich detail.

WHALE TELEMETRY

Losses over 1B ISK trigger RED alerts. Extreme kills are illuminated in GOLD.

TECHNICAL FAQ
  • Latency: Variable speeds are governed by the RedisQ API performance.
  • Privacy: No character data is stored. Our cache exists only to improve speed (and avoid a CCP phone call).
  • Domain: Currently a subdomain of Voidspark. One day? socketkill.space.
